Introduction

If someone had told me a few years ago that my journey would span continents and industries, I might have smiled skeptically. But here I am—an ambitious, resourceful professional turning data into insights and challenges into opportunities, living my American dream.

Hello, I'm Shubhi, a data enthusiast with a knack for uncovering stories hidden in numbers. Armed with a Master’s degree in Data Analytics Engineering from Northeastern University, I bring over 6 years of experience across Capital Markets, Investment Banking, Telecom, and Healthcare. My expertise lies in ETL processes, building dashboards, predictive analytics, and optimizing business solutions using tools like Python, SQL, and Tableau.

When I’m not exploring data, you’ll find me diving into a thought-provoking book or immersing myself in the fascinating stories others have to share , or discovering Boston's hidden gems. As I write this, I’m enjoying a quiet moment at my favorite café, reflecting on how far I’ve come and where I want to go next.

On my Portfolio Page, you’ll find a collection of my work—ranging from visualizations to real-world business solutions. Whether you're here to collaborate, learn, or simply connect, I hope my portfolio inspires you to see the transformative power of data. Thank you for stopping by!

How Telling Stories Through Data
